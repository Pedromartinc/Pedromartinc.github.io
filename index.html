Welcome!

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Research Center on Inequality and Social Policy, University of Bremen. In 2025, I completed my PhD at the Department of Political and Social Sciences, European University Institute.

My research focuses on the relationship between inequality and mobilization and their effects on political attitudes and behavior. I strongly rely on quantitative methodologies, often with a local focus and a political-sociological perspective. You can find below some examples of my research.
