Welcome!

I am PhD candidate at the Department of Political and Social Sciences, European University Institute.

My research focuses on the relationship between inequality and mobilization and their effects on political attitudes and behavior. Using quantitative methodologies, my research looks at patterns of political contestation, taking a local and a country-level perspective..
